9 ## Building
10 Get a copy of [Premake 5](http://premake.bitbucket.org/download.html#v5) for your OS.
11 
12 Do not forget to update the `enet` submodule:
13 
14 ```sh
15 git submodule init
16 git submodule update
17 ```
18 
19 Now run premake:
20 ```sh
21 premake5 --cc=clang gmake # Linux flavor. GCC would work as well.
22 premake5 vs2013 # Windows build.
23 ```
24 
25 Your solution/Makefile files will be in the `build` directory.
