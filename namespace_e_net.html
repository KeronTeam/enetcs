enum ENet.PacketFlags

Specifies outgoing packet transmittion behavior.

Enumerator
None 

Sequence the packet, and unless it is larger than the MTU and requires fragmentation, send it unreliably.

Reliable 

Send the packet reliably.

Unsequenced 

Allow the packet to arrive out-of-order.

NoAllocate 

Let the application, not ENet, handle memory allocation for the packet.

UnreliableFragment 

Even if an unreliable packet is larger than the MTU and requires fragmentation, send it unreliably.
